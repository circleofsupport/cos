Frequently asked questions
Common questions answered

What services does Circle of Support offer?

We provide coordinated family support for adults with developmental disabilities living at home — including future planning, care coordination, and regional center navigation.

Who is eligible for our services?

Adults with developmental disabilities receiving regional center services who live or plan to live with their family.

Do you serve Armenian-speaking families?

Yes — in both Eastern and Western Armenian dialects, with staff respectful of Armenian culture and community values.

How do I get started?

Call (323) 388-7534 or email cos.mainpage@gmail.com — we'll guide you every step of the way.
